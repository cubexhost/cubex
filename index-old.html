Welcome to
CUBEX

CubeX is a 3D animated collection of walking Cubies which will double as your personal avatar and playable character in CubeWorld.

From our P2E experience to our decentralized social media application, building is a common theme at CubeX! Enjoy the journey.

ROADMAP
PHASE ONE
CubeWorld Beta

The beginning of our open-world play to earn experience. Our beta will give you a glimpse of what's in store when it comes to CubeWorld.

PHASE TWO
CubeX Minting

7,777 Cubies have been released and are en route to the metaverse. Each Cubie will double as your personal avatar and playable character in CubeWorld.

PHASE THREE
CubeWorld Official Launch

The release of our metaverse, CubeWorld, will include 3 minigames; BedWars, SkyWars, and Laser Tag. Users will be able to play with friends and compete for $WRLD token. NFT Holders will earn higher yields of $WRLD token through scheduled events, games, and exclusive CubeX holder lobbies!

PHASE FOUR
Hubbl Beta

Our main objective is to create a decentralized social media platform catered to the NFT and crypto space where anyone can freely express their thoughts without censorship or the risk of getting shadowed. Hubbl will also include a variety of features that the NFT and crypto space already use daily, all integrated into one platform!

PHASE FIVE
Cube Store Launch

The Cube Store will contain cosmetics such as player skins, player upgrades, and other in-game accessories. The Cube Store will be accessible to all players in CubeWorld, with exclusive items for holders, and will be a limited-time shop with new items dropping each month. We will have our own in-game currency called $CUBE coin that will allow you to purchase items within the Cube Store! 

PHASE SIX
Added Game Modes

The integration of new minigames as well as full-length game modes will be added into the CubeWorld ecosystem, allowing for a larger variety of ways to earn $WRLD and be rewarded $CUBE!

PHASE SEVEN
Hubbl Application Launch

Hubbl will be available on the Apple App Store for iOS and Google Play for Android. Holders will get access to in-app benefits which will be available for public purchase. Initial application features will include 

- Wallet Connect To Log In
- Web3 User Profile Showcasing Your   NFT Collection
- Home Page To Upload Messages & Stories
- Connect With Other Web3 Users Based On   
  Specific Niches
- Top Trending Projects & News Updates
- In-App “Alpha” Groups Chats
- NFT Market Analytics
- Gas Tracker
- Hot Upcoming Drops
- Check NFT Rarities

PHASE EIGHT
Land Sale & Staking

We will have a CubeWorld land sale open to the public, where each CubeX holder will be airdropped a plot of land based on the rarity of the NFT; The more rare your CubeX is, the closer your plot will be to the community square. Each plot of land can be staked to earn our in-game currency, $CUBE coin, which can be used in the Cube Store. You will be able to build on your land and customize it to your wildest imagination!
